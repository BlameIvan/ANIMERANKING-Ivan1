Neon Genesis Evangelion

Rating: 10/10 | Perfect

Quick Summary

Neon Genesis Evangelion follows Shinji Ikari, a troubled teenager who is recruited by the organization NERV to pilot a giant bio-mechanical robot, the Evangelion, in a desperate battle against mysterious beings known as Angels. As he fights to protect humanity, Shinji struggles with his complex relationship with his father, Gendo, and his fellow pilots, Rei Ayanami and Asuka Langley Soryu. The series delves into themes of identity, isolation, and existential despair, revealing the psychological trauma of its characters amidst the backdrop of a crumbling world. Ultimately, the narrative leads to a surreal and introspective conclusion, challenging traditional storytelling conventions.
