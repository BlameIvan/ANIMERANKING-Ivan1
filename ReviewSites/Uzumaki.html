Re:Zero

Rating: 9/10 | Almost Perfect

Quick Summary

Dandadan's first season has been a wild and eccentric ride. Having read the manga, this anime is a faithful adaptation that uses animation to enhance the content of the original source material. Momo and Okarun have a fun dynamic that makes room for comedic relief, while also allowing for more heartfelt moments. The series' highstakes battles are accompanied by amazing animation and absurd enemies with even more absurd motivation. From aliens to ghosts, this show has it all. I'd say to give it a shot, and to keep a box of tissues by your side when the story gets sad.

Best moments (Beware of spoilers...DUH):

  1. Acrobat Silky's Backstory
  2. Okarun Gets Rocked
  3. Okarun Goes Full Power
