Re:Zero

Rating: 9/10 | Almost Perfect

Quick Summary

Re:Zero has an electric cast of characters that come to produce emotional moments and action packed scenes. The main character, Subaru, never fails to amaze the audience with his witty banter and amazing determination to keep moving. Adding to that is the insane world-building in this show, which can definitely be attributed to its source material being a light-novel. The show does drag just a bit in the beginning of the second season, but the end of the season more than makes up for it.

Best moments (Beware of spoilers...DUH):

  1. Betelgeuse's Insanity
  2. Subaru's Power
  3. Subaru and Beatrice vs The Great Rabbit
