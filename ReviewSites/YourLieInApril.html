Your Lie in April

Rating: 8/10 | Amazing

Quick Summary

I think I needed 3 boxes of tissues and 3 rolls of Bounty to clean up all the tears I cried after watching this. The characters are so likeable and all of their reasons for doing what they do in this show are so human and raw. The main love-interest for the main character is a person who is so loveable and so kind that the ending makes you want to wish that you never watched it. This consumed me after I watched it, I couldn't stop thinking about it. For a romance anime, this was incredibly well done.
