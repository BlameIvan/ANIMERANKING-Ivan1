Naruto

Rating: 9/10 | Near Perfect

Quick Summary

This brings back so many memories. I remember being a wee little lad watching this (to be honest it was only like 5 years ago) and falling in love with the cheesy and emotional intros. Crying at Jiraiya's death harder than I did when I found out I would need to take a second mortgage on my house to afford to keep buying anime figurines for my collection. This is a classic for a reason, this is a no-nonsense straight up show with lots emotion and worldbuilding. My only critique of the show is the final 70-50 episodes. That ENTIRE Infinte Tsukuyomi arc was so unnecessary and not at all worth my tine, which could be said about most of the filler arcs in this show. My mistake was not just skipping the filler entirely, don't make the same mistake as me
